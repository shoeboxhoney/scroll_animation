Five stages of grief

1. Denial

It feels unreal, and you cannot accept that this is actually happening.

2. Anger

You become frustrated and question why this is happening to you.

3. Bargaining

You try to find ways to reverse or change what happened, hoping for a different outcome.

4. Depression

You feel the weight of sadness as the reality of the situation sinks in.

5. Acceptance

You come to terms with the loss and begin to find a way to move forward.
